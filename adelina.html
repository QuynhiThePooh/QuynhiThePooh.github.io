Adelina

“Confusion and clutter are the failure of design, not the attributes of information.”

Adelina is an application which is meant to connect the elderly with volunteering oppurtunities. The project was the result of our task to create a product which contributes to active aging and improves the quality of life for our seniors.

The screens you see are meant for the people and NGOs who host the non-profit events.

Our goal was to create a design which conveys vigor and competence.

The calm green tones and the simple layout help you easily have an overview over all your options without being overwhelmed by the inforation.
