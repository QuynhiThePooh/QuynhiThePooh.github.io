Movie Matcher

“Don’t find customers for your products, find products for your customers.”

The app concept for this After Effects Showcase is a hybrid between the two very well known apps ‚Netflix and ‚Tinder‘. The Result is an animation which shows how this application would work.

The first step in order to create the animation was to design the screens. Since we are basing this off of already existing apps the challenge was to figure out how two iconic interfaces could harmonize and merge them into an intuitive user flow.

The screens ended up having the recognizable Netflix black and red color scheme and the swiping UI of tinder. The feel is supposed to be mainly cinematic since the purpose is movie selection. This way most posters stand out.

This is the final result: The animation had a special emphasis on timing and how moving elements feel to the user especially for repeated events such as swiping and matching. Those can easily turn into annoyances or are a tool to influence feelings of satisfaction or prioritization.
